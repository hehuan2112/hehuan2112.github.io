Video Utilization Sphere and Enrollment Analysis
To understand the video utilization patterns and the enrollment across the country, we designed and implemented this visual analytic system called VUSphere. It has three linked views: an overview with a spherical layout to show the overall video utilization, a detailed statistics view with 4 panels with 9 tabs, and a comparison view to examine different elements.

2015 - 2016
GFS: GridFS to FUSE System
To facilitate the efficiency and scalability of small files reading and writing, we designed and implemented a basic file system based on FUSE and MongoDB with refer to prior work in our team. With the characteristics of the MongoDB database, we designed a algorithm and a data structure for mapping from the tree directory structure to the key-value structure and implemented the operations specified by FUSE, such as getattr, create, write, flush, etc. In addition, I visualized the detailed r/w operation sequences to improve the performance.
WonderTek Inc., Shanghai, China

Educational Data Transfer System
In order to connnect isolated information islands between information systems of many departments, such as graduate school, department of archives, finance office, employment center etc., we designed and development this system based on SOA approach and technologies. Data in each isolated system can be synced without modification to existing systems.
Network and Information Center, Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, Shaanxi, China
2009 - 2010
Take a Photo, 1, 2, OK!
At that time, taking a ID photo was a very time-consuming part during the freshmen enrollment. It takes 2-3 minutes for each part-time photographer to take a photo that meets standard. As a result, students had to wait in a long queue. To address this issue, we developed a semi-automatic camera system that integrates campus card service, standard PC camera and student information system based on OpenCV and SOA technologies. An intern student can take a good photo within 15 seconds with just 3 mouse clicks, which greatly improves the efficiency and significantly shorten the queue length.
Network and Information Center, Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, Shaanxi, China
2008 - 2009
Self-Service Payment System
At that time, the process of paying various fees required manual services (e.g., network fee, entry fee, etc.), which were not only time-consuming but also cumbersome. We developed a desktop software and a web-based application to help students self-pay on the terminals. The detailed design and are included in my master thesis.
Network and Information Center, Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, Shaanxi, China
